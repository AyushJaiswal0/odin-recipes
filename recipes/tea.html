Tea

Description

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot water or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of an evergreen shrub native to East Asia. Tea has a stimulating effect in humans, primarily due to its caffeine content.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea Powder
  • Sugar
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add sugar, crushed ginger and tea powder in it and bowl for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  3. Add milk and boil it over medium flame until bubble starts to rise. You will see the change in color of the tea from milky shade to brown shade when it is ready.
  4. Turn off the gas and strain the tea in cups, and enjoy!
